WHO WE ARE

Exercising sustainability in an economy that seems to work against you has proven to be extremely difficult in the past decade. SHRED looks for financially friendly ways that the average person can contribute to our planet's well-being. With each issue, we open conversations that benefit both you and the planet. Sustainability does not need to take away from your individuality nor should it weigh you down – you can’t save the world but you can certainly enhance it for yourself. From fashion to food, we can teach you how to stay soft: on the wallet, on the mind, on the planet.
