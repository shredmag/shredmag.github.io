WHAT IS SOFT SUSTAINABILITY?

Soft sustainability is the idea of making sustainability fun, easy and guilt-free. We want to make it about not only taking care of the planet but also taking care of you (and your wallet). Sustainability should be accessible to all, whether or not you have money to spare and space to use.



Soft sustainability is our gentle answer to eco-grief. When you’re looking at the state of our planet, it sometimes feels like the weight of the world is on your shoulders to fix it. We know we can’t do it, especially alone. Together, we’ll take small steps to do what we can and change our world bit by bit.
