GIVE YOUR CLOTHES A SECOND-LIFE

How to Style Sustainably 



REUSING AND RECYCLING

The simplest way to keep sustainable and fashionable is to reuse outfits. There’s a societal taboo around outfit repeaters but let’s be realistic: we’re all guilty of it anyway. Uniqueness is valuable, but so is character - and what’s more character-like than wearing the same things over and over again?

Nevertheless, the best way to avoid a Lizzie McGuire accusation is to span out your repeats. Give it a week or something around that, let people forget. Change a minor detail about it, the accessories, the socks, how you wear your hair – that alone already gives you a fresh, unseen look.

DIY AND DECORATION

A little trickier than outfit-repeating is to hit edit on your closet. Styles and trends change, so do our bodies and our tastes. A shirt too big is only a few stitches away from cinching you just right; a skirt too small is a pull away from being a very unique tube top. Sewing might be a bit of a task to learn but it’s a skill you’ll be able to carry with you throughout your life. Even simple tricks like using iron-on tapes can make a trip to a charity shop come out with a lot more potential. There is nothing a pair of scissors aren't capable of – turn your crew neck into an off-shoulder piece, your starchy jeans into summer shorts.

BACKWARDS, FORWARDS, AND ALL OVER

Swap your v-neck for a low-back by flipping it around! Your clothes don’t come with instructions for a reason – who says you can’t button your cardigan on backwards and give yourself a new jumper? The laws of physics don’t apply to fabric (at least in the world of fashion). Skip buying that bandeau top and squeeze into that classic mini-skirt. No one will be able to tell the difference.

LAYERS AND LUXURY

Learning layers is the key to a special closet. Don’t be afraid of overdoing it! Fashion is whatever you say it is. Dress over skirts over stockings over tights -  anything can be layered. Giving yourself more to wear can give a look a lot more personality. It doesn’t need to be bulky layers either - you can layer choker over necklace, coat over cardigan. A great way not to freeze in the winters too. 


https://vt.tiktok.com/ZSFmBcxCQ/


SCARVES ARE YOUR BEST FRIENDS

There is nothing more flexible in the fashion world than a scarf. You can wear it for warmth around your neck or turn it into a more sophisticated shawl situation. Cross a scarf across your chest and you’ve got yourself a new top! Cross it across your hips and you’ve got a skirt – nothing screams versatility like a scarf.
